# Repair Ticket 4107: Hot Restart Stall After Roadside Battery Swap

Vehicle: Ardent Courier route van 18

## Customer description

Driver says the van was jump-started twice and then received a roadside battery replacement. Since then it starts fine in the morning, but after a lunch stop it stumbles, catches itself, and once stalled backing out of the parking space.

## Technician notes

- Fault memory showed a stored but not current P17A2.
- No fuel smell was present.
- Fuel pressure stayed normal during the warm restart check.
- Battery tray had fresh tool marks and the throttle connector lock tab was not fully seated.

## Resolution

Reseated the throttle connector, ran idle relearn, completed three heat-soak restarts, and returned the van with no repeat stall.
