# Repair Manual FS-88: Low Rail Pressure and Persistent P17A2

## Applies to

Ardent Courier 2.0T delivery vans.

## Use this procedure when

- P17A2 remains active under load.
- The customer reports strong fuel odor or sulfur smell.
- Hot or cold starts both show rough running.

## Diagnostic path

1. Measure rail pressure during crank and at 2500 RPM.
2. Inspect the pump control module for heat damage.
3. Perform injector balance test.
4. Replace the fuel pump module only if rail pressure stays below specification.

## Not the usual fit for this procedure

If the only trigger is battery replacement followed by a warm restart stall, inspect throttle adaptation first before replacing fuel components.
