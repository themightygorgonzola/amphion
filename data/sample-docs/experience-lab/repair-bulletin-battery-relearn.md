# Service Bulletin SB-17A2-04: Hot Restart Stall After Battery Service

Vehicle line: Ardent Courier 2.0T delivery vans

## Condition

Some vehicles may exhibit a rough idle or brief stall during a hot restart after battery replacement, jump starting, or battery disconnection during unrelated service. The malfunction indicator may log a transient P17A2 torque-adaptation fault.

## Distinguishing pattern

- Symptom appears after the battery has been disconnected or replaced.
- Complaint is strongest after a 10 to 20 minute heat soak.
- Fuel pressure usually remains in the normal range.
- The code often clears after restart and may not return on a cold start.

## Service action

1. Inspect the throttle body connector and harness routing around the battery tray.
2. Perform the idle relearn procedure in section EM-142.
3. Update engine calibration package CAL-17A2-R to the latest revision.

## When not to use this bulletin

Do not use this procedure as the primary repair path when the complaint includes strong fuel odor, low rail pressure, or a persistent P17A2 under load. Those symptoms point to the fuel supply diagnostic branch.
