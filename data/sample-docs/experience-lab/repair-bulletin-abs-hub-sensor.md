# Service Bulletin BR-21C1-07: ABS Light After Front Hub Service

Vehicle line: Ardent Courier 2.0T and Astera VX-18 vans

## Condition

Some vehicles may show ABS and traction lamps after front hub, knuckle, or wheel bearing service. The complaint usually starts on the first road test above 15 to 20 mph.

## Distinguishing pattern

- Brake pedal feel remains normal.
- One front wheel speed PID drops out intermittently.
- The issue starts immediately after hub or bearing work.

## Service action

1. Inspect wheel speed sensor seating and harness routing at the knuckle.
2. Verify encoder ring orientation and air gap with procedure BR-204.
3. Reclip the harness away from the strut flange and splash shield.

## When not to use this bulletin

Do not use this path when the pedal sinks after hydraulic service, fluid was opened recently, or no wheel-speed dropout is present.
