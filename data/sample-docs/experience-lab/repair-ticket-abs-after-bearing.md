# Repair Ticket 5521: ABS Event After Right Front Bearing Replacement

## Customer concern

ABS and traction lights return within one block of leaving the shop after right front bearing replacement. Brake pedal feel is normal, but scan data shows the right front speed signal dropping to zero above 22 mph.

## Resolution

The wheel speed sensor was not fully seated after rust scale stayed under the flange. The harness was also clipped behind the splash shield and rubbed during steering sweep. Cleaning the mounting face, reseating the sensor, and reclipping the harness fixed the issue.
