# Repair Manual BR-204: Front Wheel Speed Sensor Air Gap and Encoder Ring

## Applies to

Ardent Courier 2.0T and Astera VX-18 front hub assemblies.

## Use this procedure when

- ABS or traction lights appear after hub or bearing service.
- One wheel speed signal drops out above parking-lot speed.
- Brake pedal feel stays normal.

## Procedure

1. Remove the wheel speed sensor and inspect for debris under the mounting flange.
2. Confirm the encoder ring faces the sensor and is not installed backwards.
3. Measure air gap and verify it is within specification.
4. Route the harness with full steering sweep and suspension travel checked.

## Escalate to hydraulic diagnostics if

- The pedal is soft or sinks under steady pressure.
- The brake system was opened for bleeding or caliper replacement.
- No wheel-speed dropout appears in live data.
