# Shop Note: Battery Tray Harness Disturbance Pattern

## Pattern summary

When the battery tray is removed for battery or fuse-box service, the nearby throttle harness can be tugged just enough to leave the connector latched shallow. The vehicle may then show an intermittent warm restart stumble with a transient P17A2.

## What makes this easy to miss

- Cold starts are usually normal.
- Basic fuel checks often look fine.
- The complaint sounds like a fuel issue until the battery-service history is mentioned.

## Practical reminder

Ask whether the battery, battery tray, or fuse box was touched before you start replacing fuel parts.
