# Repair Ticket 4192: Persistent P17A2 With Fuel Odor Under Load

Vehicle: Ardent Courier route van 27

## Customer description

Driver reports rough running on both cold and hot starts, sulfur smell in the cabin, and loss of power climbing grades.

## Technician notes

- P17A2 returned immediately during the road test.
- Rail pressure dipped below target on crank and under acceleration.
- No recent battery service was reported.

## Resolution

Followed the fuel supply diagnostic path, replaced the pump control module and fuel pump assembly, and verified normal pressure afterward.
