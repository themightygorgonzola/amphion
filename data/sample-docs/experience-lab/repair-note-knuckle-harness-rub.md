# Shop Note: Knuckle Harness Rub After Hub Jobs

## Pattern summary

After hub or bearing service, the front wheel speed harness can get trapped behind the splash shield or pulled tight at full lock. The first road test then shows an intermittent wheel speed dropout with normal pedal feel.

## Practical reminder

If the complaint begins right after knuckle work, inspect sensor seating and harness routing before opening the hydraulic system.
