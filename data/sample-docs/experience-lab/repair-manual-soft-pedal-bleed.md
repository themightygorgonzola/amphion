# Repair Manual HY-17: Soft Pedal After Hydraulic Service

## Applies to

Ardent Courier and Astera VX-18 brake hydraulic systems.

## Use this procedure when

- The pedal sinks or feels spongy after caliper, hose, or brake line service.
- The hydraulic circuit was opened or fluid was bled recently.
- Wheel speed data remains stable.

## Diagnostic path

1. Inspect for external leaks.
2. Bleed the brake system in the specified sequence.
3. Verify caliper slide movement and pad seating.
4. Recheck pedal height with engine running.

## Not the usual fit for this procedure

If ABS or traction warnings begin only after hub service and one wheel speed signal drops out, inspect the encoder gap and harness routing first.
