# Repair Manual EM-142: Idle Relearn and Throttle Adaptation

## Applies to

Ardent Courier 2.0T and Astera VX-18 gasoline engines.

## Use this procedure when

- P17A2 appears after battery service or ECM memory loss.
- Hot restart stumble occurs without low fuel pressure.
- The throttle body or battery tray was recently disturbed.

## Procedure

1. Confirm battery voltage is stable above 12.4V.
2. Inspect the throttle body connector for partial unseating or pin drag.
3. Start the engine and allow idle relearn for 8 minutes with all accessories off.
4. Perform three warm restarts after a 10 minute heat soak.
5. If the fault does not return, release the vehicle.

## Escalate to fuel diagnostics if

- Fuel odor is present in the engine bay.
- Rail pressure drops below specification during crank.
- P17A2 remains active under load after relearn.
