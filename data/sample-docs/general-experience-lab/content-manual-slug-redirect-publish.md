# Publishing Manual KB-301: Slug Redirect and Link Repair Checklist

## Applies to

Knowledge base articles, landing pages, and campaign destinations.

## Use this procedure when

- A page rename or move caused old links to fail.
- Campaign buttons still point to the old slug.
- Editors can open the renamed page directly, but cross-links return 404.

## Procedure

1. Register redirects from old slugs to the new canonical path.
2. Rebuild cached navigation and link maps.
3. Update campaign buttons and article references.
4. Verify the redirect checklist before republishing.

## Escalate to email delivery operations if

- The destination pages work normally.
- Subscribers are missing messages because of bounce suppression.
- Domain reputation or suppression lists are the main issue.
