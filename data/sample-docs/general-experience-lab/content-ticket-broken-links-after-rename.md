# Publishing Ticket 6284: Broken Internal Links After Knowledge Base Rename

## Editorial report

An article was renamed for a new campaign, but internal links and CTA buttons still pointed to the old slug. Readers hit 404 pages even though the new article path was live.

## Resolution

Editorial ops registered redirects for the old slug, rebuilt the link map, and updated campaign CTA references. All links resolved after the publish checklist was rerun.
