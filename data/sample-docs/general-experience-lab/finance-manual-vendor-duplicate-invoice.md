# Finance Manual AP-221: Duplicate Vendor Invoice Reversal and Remittance Correction

## Applies to

Supplier invoices processed through accounts payable.

## Use this procedure when

- A vendor invoice posted or paid twice.
- Bank details were changed near the payment run.
- The remittance does not match the PO or invoice schedule.

## Procedure

1. Freeze the duplicate payment batch.
2. Validate bank details against approved vendor master changes.
3. Reverse the duplicate posting and issue corrected remittance.
4. Reconcile the PO and invoice schedule.

## Not the usual fit for this procedure

If the issue is an employee reimbursement blocked on missing travel receipts, use the expense receipt exception flow instead.
