# Finance Ticket 5594: Duplicate Vendor Payment After Bank Detail Update

## Accounts payable report

The supplier invoice was paid twice after a bank detail change was approved on the same day as the payment run. The remittance advice no longer matched the PO schedule.

## Resolution

Accounts payable froze the second batch, validated the vendor master update, reversed the duplicate posting, and reissued corrected remittance against the proper PO schedule.
