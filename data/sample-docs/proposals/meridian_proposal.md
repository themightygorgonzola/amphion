## Scope of Work

### Phase 1 — Pre-Development Advisory ($60,000)
- Site feasibility and zoning analysis
- Market demand study coordination
- Development pro forma review
- Lender introduction and pre-qualification
- Design team selection support

### Phase 2 — Development Management ($120,000)
- Full-time owner's representative services during design and permitting
- Contractor selection and bid process management
- Contract negotiation and execution support
- Schedule and budget oversight
- Monthly reporting to ownership group

### Phase 3 — Construction Administration ($60,000)
- Owner's representative through construction
- Draw request review and approval
- Change order analysis and negotiation
- Punch list and closeout management
- Final lender reporting

---

## Key Win Factors

1. **Fixed fee with no surprises** — Meridian had a bad experience with hourly billing on a prior project. Our fixed-fee structure with clear phase gates was the decisive factor.
2. **Phased structure** — Client could exit after Phase 1 if the project didn't proceed. This reduced perceived risk significantly.
3. **Local market knowledge** — Our familiarity with the specific submarket and prior relationships with the permitting department resonated with the client.
4. **Fast turnaround** — Delivered proposal within 5 business days; competitors took 3 weeks.
