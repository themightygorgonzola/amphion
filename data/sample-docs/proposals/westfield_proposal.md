## Opportunity Background

Westfield is developing a 6.5-acre mixed-use site adjacent to the Riverside Transit Hub. The project includes 320 multifamily units, 40,000 sq ft of ground-floor retail, and 15,000 sq ft of office space. They are seeking a development advisory partner to manage complexity across the multi-phase project.

## Our Proposed Approach

### Advisory Structure
Full-scope development advisory from pre-development through construction closeout. Fixed fee of $180,000 covers:

- **Pre-Development** ($45,000): Entitlement strategy, design team selection, pro forma validation, lender selection and financing strategy
- **Development Management** ($90,000): Full owner's rep services through design development and permitting; contractor procurement; contract negotiation
- **Construction** ($45,000): Construction oversight, draw management, change order control, closeout

### Differentiators in This Proposal
- Transit-oriented development expertise: we've closed 3 TOD projects in the last 5 years
- Existing relationship with the city's transit authority — can facilitate coordination meetings
- Retail tenant advisory capability in-house — we can drive occupancy from day one
- Proven approach to phased delivery that protects construction financing covenants
